Oreo Milkshake



Description

Milkshakes are a classic American frosty treat. A creamy combination of ice cream and milk.

With the combination of these two, you have the base for a imense variety of flavors. Here's a recipe for an Oreo milkshake

Ingredients

  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk (preferably whole milk)
  • 8 Oreo cookies (or other chocolate sandwich cookies)
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate sauce
  • 2 Oreo cookies, garnish

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. the vanilla ice cream, milk, 8 Oreo cookies, and the chocolate sauce in a blender and puree until smooth.
  2. Place the vanilla ice cream, milk, 8 Oreo cookies, and the chocolate sauce in a blender and puree until smooth.
  3. Make the Homemade Pizza Sauce.
  4. Crush the remaining 2 Oreo cookies for the garnish by placing them in a zip-close plastic bag and pounding on them a few times with a rolling pin until they crumble.
  5. Pour the milkshakes into 2 tall glasses and top each with the crushed Oreo cookie crumbs. Garnish with a straw.
